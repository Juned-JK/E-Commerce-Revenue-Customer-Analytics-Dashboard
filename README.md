# 🛒 E-Commerce Revenue & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This end-to-end Data Analytics project analyzes e-commerce sales data to uncover insights related to revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow:

**Data Cleaning (Python) → EDA (MySQL) → Visualization (Matplotlib & Seaborn) → Interactive Dashboards (Tableau & Power BI)**


## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance
* Identify top-performing products and countries
* Track monthly revenue and customer growth
* Understand customer retention behavior
* Compare repeat vs one-time customers
* Discover revenue contribution of top customers
* Evaluate pricing and sales relationships

## 📂 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning with Python

* Removed cancelled orders
* Handled missing values
* Created Month, Year, and Quarter columns
* Calculated Revenue metrics
* Exported cleaned dataset

### 2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (MySQL)

Performed SQL analysis including:

* Monthly Revenue Trends
* Top Revenue Countries
* Customer Purchase Behavior
* Product Performance Analysis
* Repeat vs One-Time Customers
* Top Customer Contribution Analysis

### 3️⃣ Data Visualization

Created visualizations using:

* Line Charts
* Bar Charts
* Scatter Plots
* Pie/Donut Charts
* Customer Analysis Charts

### 4️⃣ Dashboard Development

Built interactive dashboards in:

* Tableau
* Power BI


# 📊 Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

* Total Revenue reached **$8.91M**
* Peak revenue occurred in **November ($1.16M)**
* Q4 generated the highest sales performance

### Customer Insights

* 4,339 unique customers
* 65.57% customers were repeat buyers
* Strong customer retention indicates customer loyalty

### Geographic Analysis

* United Kingdom generated **$7.31M revenue**
* UK contributed the majority of total sales

### Product Analysis

* PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE was among the highest revenue-generating products
* Several products generated minimal revenue despite being sold

### Customer Concentration

* Top 10 customers contributed **17.26%** of total revenue
* A small group of customers drives a significant share of sales
